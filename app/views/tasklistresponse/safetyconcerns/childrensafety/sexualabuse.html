Safety concerns Have the children ever been sexually abused?

A child is sexually abused when they are forced or persuaded to take part in sexual activities, including online. It may not necessarily involve a high level of violence, and the child may or may not be aware of what's happening.

There are 2 types of child sexual abuse:

  • contact abuse (where an abuser makes physical contact)
  • non-contact abuse (for example grooming or exploitation)
