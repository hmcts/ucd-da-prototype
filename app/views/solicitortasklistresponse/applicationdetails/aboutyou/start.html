Keeping your contact details private

A copy of your response will be sent to the other people named in this application (the applicants). This will include your contact details.

If you believe the other people in the case pose a risk to you or the children, you can ask the court to keep your contact details private.

Check your documents

You must make sure that documents used in the case do not contain the information you want to keep private.

This includes documents you are filling in now, and documents completed in the future.

You must also check documents that are received from other people - for example, medical reports or financial statements. The court staff will not be able to check the documents for you.
