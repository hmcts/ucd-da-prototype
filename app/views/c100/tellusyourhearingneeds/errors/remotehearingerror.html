Attending the court

Would you be able to take part in hearings by video and phone?

If your case goes to a hearing, it can take place either:

  • in person, in a room at a venue ('face-to-face')
  • by video (where you can join from a place suitable to you)
  • by phone

Some hearings use a combination of these methods. The approach taken will be decided by a judge.
