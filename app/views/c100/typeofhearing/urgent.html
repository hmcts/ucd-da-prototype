Request an urgent first hearing

Your initial hearing will usually take place 5 or 6 weeks after the court has processed your application.

You can ask for the hearing to take place sooner if there is a risk factor in your case.

Examples of risk factors include:

  • a risk of harm to you on the children
  • a risk that the children will be abducted
  • loss of evidence in the case

See the list of potential risk factors.

! Warning Only ask for an earlier hearing if you have a good reason to do so. Otherwise, your request will be denied and the hearing will take place in the usual period of time.
