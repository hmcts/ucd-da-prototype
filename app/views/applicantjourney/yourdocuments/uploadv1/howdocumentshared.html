How your documents will be shared

! Warning When you upload a document, it will be shared with the other people in the case.

If there is information you do not want to share, remove it from the document. If this is not possible, you can ask the court to restrict who can see the document.

The court will only agree to restrict who can see the document if:

  • there is a good reason not to share the document, for example safety concerns
  • the document is not something the judge needs to see
  • an address that needs to be kept private is included in the document
